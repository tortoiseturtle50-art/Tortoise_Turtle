Official Husbandry Standards

1. Thermal Precision: Every habitat must maintain a strict hot-to-cool gradient as defined by species biology.

2. UVB Calibration: We require T5 High Output lighting. All bulbs must be replaced every 6-9 months regardless of visible light.

3. The 90-Day Stable: Residents are monitored for 90 days post-arrival to ensure nutritional and biological stability before rehoming.

4. Return Privilege: Adopters agree that if they can no longer care for the animal, it returns to T.T.A.R. immediately.

Subject: Nova (Albino Green Slider)

Water Temp: 25°C - 27°C
Basking Temp: 32°C
UVB Lighting: T5 HO 5.0 (Gradient required)
Nutrition: Daily (High Protein Focus)

Special Sensitivity Advisory

As an Albino morph, Nova lacks protective pigment. Ensure water is tinted with Indian Almond Leaves to protect her eyes from glare. Do not use bright, direct spotlighting; utilize a "mottled shade" approach in her enclosure to prevent skin sensitivity.
